Quiz Político

An interactive AI-driven utility designed for the Portuguese public to explore their political leanings through a comprehensive questionnaire that maps responses across the political spectrum.

This project prioritizes civic utility over self-promotion. Rather than a display of manual coding, it is an exploration of how AI tools can be creatively applied to solve complex social questions and help users navigate political ideologies with clarity and neutrality.

Open to suggestions or corrections regarding party axis positioning to maintain total neutrality and accuracy in political representation.
